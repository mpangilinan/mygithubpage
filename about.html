My 30-second elevator pitch

I'm a fourth year undergraduate at the University of California, Berkeley studying Cognitive Science with a minor in Computer Science. I have big dreams of contributing towards the advancement of Artificial Intelligence, but until then I'm passionate about bridging the gap between people and technology in any way I can.

I'm currently interested in software engineering and the applications of AI to everyday technology. This summer I interned at a telecommunications corporation where I applied Markov Decision Processes and Naive Bayes to analyze user habits to predict and suggest what users should watch (and when they should watch it).

While my website is still under construction, check out my blog to read more about my adventures and insights.
